Can you meet this legal eyesight standard for driving?

More about the legal standard for driving

The legal eyesight standard means that:

  • you must be able to read a car number plate from 20 metres
  • you must not have been told by a doctor or optician that your eyesight is currently worse than 6/12 (decimal 0.5) on the Snellen scale

If you are in any doubt, you should discuss with your optician or doctor. If required, you may wear glasses or corrective lenses to meet both of these standards.
