Can you meet the legal eyesight standard for driving?

View the legal eyesight standard
  • The legal eyesight standard means that you must be able to read a car number plate from 20 metres.

  • Also, you must not have been told by a doctor or optician that your eyesight is currently worse than 6/12 (decimal 0.5) on the Snellen scale.

  • If you are in any doubt, you should discuss with your optician or doctor.

  • If required, you may wear glasses or corrective lenses to meet both of these standards.
